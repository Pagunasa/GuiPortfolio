About me

Hi I'm Gui! A game programmer and dessigner from Barcelona, since I was little I love the world of videogames and I've always wanted to learn how to create my own games. I start playing with gamemaker at 14 and created my first videogame when I was 17 as a final project for high school, it was a very simple legend of zelda like game with a top-down camera. I graduated from UPF BSM studying the master Videogames: Design, Creation and Programming and from UPF where I study Computer Engineering.

In this website you'll be able to see some of the projects that I've have the oportunity to work and some others that are some personal things just to experiment and have fun. I have a strong perseverance and motivation in learning new tings and improve and my big imagination allways brings me some crazy ideas. I allways like to polish my work to achieve the best possible version.
